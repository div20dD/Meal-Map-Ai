# MealMap AI

MealMap AI is an intelligent meal-planning application that helps users decide what to eat based on ingredients available at home, while promoting healthier and more budget-friendly lifestyle choices.

The app uses AI to scan a user’s refrigerator, analyze available food items, and generate personalized 7-day meal plans for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. It considers BMI, age, dietary goals, budget, and nutritional needs such as calories, protein, carbs, and fats.

MealMap AI also suggests healthier alternatives to junk food, reduces food waste by using existing ingredients, and recommends new grocery items for variety. A unique feature of the app is its Future Health Insight, which shows users how their eating habits today can impact their long-term health outcomes.
